3.1 What is Direct Marketing in the AI B2B Context  
Direct marketing means reaching specific, identified decision -makers with targeted, 
personalised outreach — as opposed to mass adverti sing. For Ample.ai, this means reaching 
clinic owners, real estate agency directors, hotel GMs, and similar business leaders through 
channels they already use: LinkedIn, WhatsApp, email, and phone.  
3.2 LinkedIn Direct Outreach (Most Powerful B2B Channel)  
Strategy Framework  
LinkedIn is the single most effective direct marketing channel for Ample.ai's B2B sales. India 
has 110 million LinkedIn users — the second largest user base globally. Decision -makers (clinic 
owners, hotel GMs, real estate directors, NBFC heads) are accessible, and the platform allows 
hyper -targeted prospecting.  
Week -by-week launch plan for LinkedIn outreach:  
▶ Week 1: Optimise the Ample.ai company page and founder profiles. Post 3 pieces of 
content (problem -focused, not sales -focused). Defin e target personas and build prospect 
lists using LinkedIn Sales Navigator.  
▶ Week 2: Begin connection requests — 30–50/day per team member. Personalise every 
request with a 1 -sentence observation about their business. No pitch in the connection 
request.  
▶ Week  3: After connection acceptance (typically 30 –40%), send the first message — a 
value -add, not a pitch. Share a relevant case study or stat.  
▶ Week 4+: Follow up with a soft call -to-action: 'Would a 15 -minute demo make sense this 
week?'
